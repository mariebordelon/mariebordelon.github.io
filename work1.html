Why I Serve

Volunteering for
my passions
and for my town

During the fall of my junior year, my AP Environmental Science class helped design and plant a rain garden in commemoration of Ms. Allen. This project was a memorable experience and an amazing opportunity to appreciate the people in my community.


My passion for the STEM field has grown since moving to Rogers, Arkansas. After joining the Heritage robotics team, I started to become a frequent volunteer at Frank Tillery Elementary’s robotics program. I feel engulfed with joy when I am able to help young kids realize their passions just as I did here in Rogers


Rogers Heritage High School’s cross country program has helped me develop an appreciation for running and the outdoors. Volunteering to organize meets for middle and elementary schools has given me the opportunity to share and spread my appreciation for the sport to the younger generations.
